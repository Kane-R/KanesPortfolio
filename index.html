My Philosophy

I always keep in mind that the end user is a person looking at a screen and nowadays that screen is likely to be on a mobile phone but could be any size screen or device ranging from mobile to tablet to desktop to 75inch loungeroom TV to 10m wide outdoor digital display.

I also keep in mind that people's attention is a valuable and hard-faught comodity so content needs to not only be easilly accessable, but it needs to capture and maintain their attention. Content needs to be compelling, easy to consume and as entertaining as possible while staying on point like a laser.
